The Shift

Vas a recordar
el día exacto.

You'll remember
the exact day.

No pasa poco a poco. Pasa de golpe, y te vas a acordar de cuándo. La primera vez que levantas un peso que jurabas imposible. La primera vez que tu cuerpo hace lo que le pediste sin negociar. Algo en tu forma de verte, cambia y no habrá vuelta atrás.

It doesn't happen slowly. It hits all at once, and you'll know exactly when. The first time you lift something you swore you couldn't. The first time your body does what you asked without negotiating. Something shifts in how you see yourself, and there's no going back.

Ese día tu cuerpo deja de ser el problema. Deja de ser algo que tienes que corregir, esconder, y pasa a ser algo que construyes. Que funciona. Que hace lo que le pides.

Y un día lo sientes. Sin que nadie te lo confirme. 

That day your body stops being the problem. Stops being something you fix, something you hide, and becomes something you build. That works. That does what you ask.

And one day you just feel it. Without anyone confirming it for you.

Capable. Not as a concept. As something you've felt and can't unfeel.
